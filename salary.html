Method

How the estimate works

  1. National Pension is calculated at 4.75% of monthly salary.
  2. Health Insurance is calculated at 3.595% of monthly salary.
  3. Long-Term Care Insurance is calculated at 9.448% of employee health insurance.
  4. Income Tax is pulled from the monthly tax chart for 1 dependent.
  5. Local Income Tax is calculated at 10% of income tax.
  6. Net pay equals monthly salary minus total deductions.
Intended use

Who this tool is for

This estimator is designed for foreign ESL instructors in South Korea who want a fast monthly payroll estimate based on base salary only.

It is helpful for budgeting, comparing job offers, and getting a simple preview of likely monthly deductions.
